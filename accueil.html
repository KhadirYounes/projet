Congratulations on your upcoming graduation! If you're on the hunt for a graduation theme idea that's both unique and creative, you've come to the right place

To get started, simply click on the "Thèmes Proposés" link located on the left-hand side of the page. This will take you to our selection of carefully curated theme ideas
Once you've found a theme that resonates with you, it's time to make it official. We recommend registering your choice with the administration and your professor (framer) to ensure that your theme is properly recognized and incorporated into the ceremony.
To do this, click on the "Etudiants" link and follow the instructions to register your chosen theme. With your theme officially registered, you can sit back, relax, and prepare to celebrate your graduation in style
